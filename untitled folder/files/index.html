How FarFinder Works

🌍 Multi-Modal Search

We find flights from all nearby airports and add the cost of ground transport (bus, train, cab). Often you can save money flying from a nearby airport and taking a bus to the original destination.

📍 Smart Radius

Adjust the search radius from 2 km (just your city) to 600 km (entire region). More airports = more options = better deals.

💰 Full Price Breakdown

Every route card shows flight price + ground transport cost = total. You're never guessing.

📅 Calendar & Flexible Dates

See prices across an entire month or search flexible dates to find the cheapest travel window.

🔗 Direct Booking

One-click links to popular booking sites. We work with data, not bookings.
